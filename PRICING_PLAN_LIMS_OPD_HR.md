### 🚀 What's Included?

#### 1. 🏥 OPD Management (Outpatient Department)

- **Smart Appointments:** Real-time scheduling, queue management, and follow-up
  tracking.
- **Digital Prescriptions (Rx):** Create professional prescriptions in seconds
  with templates and medication history.
- **Patient Health Records (EMR):** Secure storage of patient history, vitals,
  allergies, and past visits.
- **Doctor Desk:** Dedicated interface for doctors to manage consultations and
  clinical notes.

#### 2. 🧪 LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System)

- **Test Management:** comprehensive master catalog for Pathology, Radiology,
  and other diagnostic tests.
- **Result Entry & Reporting:** Easy breakdown of test results with
  auto-calculated reference ranges.
- **Smart Reports:** Generate PDF reports automatically with branding and
  digital signatures.
- **Sample Tracking:** Track sample status from collection to reporting.

#### 3. 👥 HR & Staff Management

- **Role-Based Access:** Granular permissions for Doctors, Receptionists, Lab
  Technicians, and Pharmacists.
- **Staff Profiles:** Manage complete details of your workforce.
- **Duty & Shift Management:** (If applicable) Track staff availability and
  schedules.
- **Performance Tracking:** Insight into staff activity and patient handling
  volume.

#### 4. 💊 Pharmacy & Inventory

- **Stock Management:** Real-time tracking of medicine batches, expiry dates,
  and quantities.
- **Dispensing:** Quick dispensing linked directly to patient visits.
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Never run out of critical medicines with automated
  warnings.
- **Purchase & Supplier Management:** Manage vendor details and inward stock
  receipts.

#### 5. 💳 Billing & Finance

- **Integrated Billing:** Generate invoices for Consultations, Lab Tests, and
  Pharmacy items in a single click.
- **Payment Tracking:** Record Cash, UPI, Card, and pending payments.
- **Refund Management:** Handled structured refunds and cancellations.
- **Daily Collection Reports:** End-of-day financial summaries to prevent
  leakage.

#### 6. ✨ Premium Integrations (Included)

- **WhatsApp Automation:** Automatically send Prescriptions, Invoices, and Lab
  Reports to patients via WhatsApp.
- **Visual Trends:** Trend graphs for patient vitals and lab results over time.
- **Secure Cloud Storage:** Bank-grade security for all medical records and
  PDFs.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** (If enabled) Smart analysis of medical data for
  better clinical decisions.
